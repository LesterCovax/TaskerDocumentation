Bluetooth Connection

Do actions related to the Bluetooth Connection with another device.

There are multiple actions you can take here:

Connect
Will try to force connection between your phone and the selected Bluetooth Device.
The bluetooth device has to already be paired with your phone for this to work.
Disconnect
Will try to disconnect your phone and the selected Bluetooth Device.
The bluetooth device has to already be paired with your phone for this to work.
Pair
Will try to pair with this unpaired device
Unpair
Will remove the Bluetooth device from your paired device list.
